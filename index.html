Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Nairobi?+

Yes! We deliver to all 47 counties. WhatsApp us your county for an exact quote within 30 minutes.

Can I order a custom size or colour?+

Absolutely! Send a description, photo, or sketch via WhatsApp. Quote within 24 hours, turnaround 7–21 days.

How do I pay?+

M-Pesa Paybill 522533, cash on delivery (Nairobi), or bank transfer. 30% deposit confirms your order.

What if furniture arrives damaged?+

WhatsApp us a photo. We arrange free replacement or repair within 48 hours — no questions asked.

Do you offer layby or installments?+

Yes! 3-month interest-free layby. Pay 30% to reserve, split the rest over 2 more payments.

Can I visit your showroom?+

Yes! Westlands Square, Ring Road, 1st Floor Unit 12. Mon–Sat 8am–7pm, Sunday 10am–5pm.
