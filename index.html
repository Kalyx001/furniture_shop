Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Nairobi? +

Yes! We deliver to all 47 counties in Kenya. Delivery cost and timeline depends on location. WhatsApp us with your county and we'll give you an exact quote within 30 minutes.

Can I order a custom size or colour? +

Absolutely! Custom orders are our speciality. Send us a description, photo, or sketch via WhatsApp and we'll get you a quote within 24 hours. Turnaround time is 7–21 days.

How do I pay? +

We accept M-Pesa (Paybill 522533), cash on delivery (Nairobi only), and bank transfer. Pay a 30% deposit to confirm your order and the balance on delivery.

What if my furniture arrives damaged? +

All our deliveries are insured. If any item arrives damaged, WhatsApp us a photo and we'll arrange a free replacement or repair within 48 hours — no questions asked.

Do you offer layby or installments? +

Yes! We offer a 3-month interest-free layby plan. Pay 30% to reserve your item, then split the rest over 2 more payments. Item is delivered after full payment.

Can I visit your showroom? +

Yes! Our showroom is at Westlands Square, Ring Road, 1st Floor Unit 12. Open Mon–Sat 8am–7pm, Sunday 10am–5pm. WhatsApp us before visiting!
