Our Story

Furniture Built
for Kenyan Homes

Since 2016, Nairobi Luxe Furniture has been crafting and sourcing premium furniture that balances style with durability. Our showroom in Westlands has served over 500 families across Kenya.

✅

Quality Guaranteed

Every piece comes with a 1-year warranty. We only stock durable, locally-tested materials.

🚚

Nairobi-Wide Delivery

Free delivery within Nairobi CBD & estates. Nationwide delivery available at a fee.

💳

M-Pesa Accepted

Pay deposit via M-Pesa Paybill. Easy layby options available on select items.

🎨

Custom Orders Welcome

Need a specific colour, size or design? We do custom orders — just WhatsApp us.

Shipping & Delivery
